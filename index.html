Train Safety Guidelines

Welcome, operators! Keeping our train network safe is our top priority. Please review these guidelines:

  • Signal Lights: Always obey signal lights. Green means go, yellow means proceed with caution, and red means stop.
  • Right of Way: Trains approaching from the right have the right of way. Yield to them.
  • Speed Limits: Adhere to posted speed limits. Slow down in curves and intersections.
  • Safe Distances: Maintain a safe following distance. Avoid tailgating other trains.
  • Emergency Procedures: Familiarize yourself with emergency protocols. Know the location of emergency stops and safe zones.
  • Communication: Use the radio to communicate with other operators and control.
  • Regular Checks: Perform regular checks on your train's systems. Report any malfunctions immediately.

Remember, safety is everyone's responsibility. Let's work together to prevent accidents and keep our network running smoothly.
